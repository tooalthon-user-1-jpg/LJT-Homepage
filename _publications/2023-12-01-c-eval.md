C-Eval is a comprehensive evaluation suite with more than 13,000 multiple-choice questions spanning 52 diverse subjects in four broad categories: Humanities, Social Sciences, Natural Sciences, and Engineering. Questions are specifically designed to test the competencies of AI systems (and humans) trained on Chinese-speaking populations'
